Benchpress: Flat

Easily the most available exercise on the strength floor. The benchpress may be done at any of the assisted weight stations or on the free flat benches on the center of the gym.

Benchpress: Inclined

Although slightly less available than the flat benchpress, the inclined bench press is still one of the most available exercises. There are 35-45 degree benches as well as negatively inclined benches.
